DogeTunnel

DogeTunnel is a low cost virtual private network (VPN) backing onto our custom crypto-currency payment and account keeping service. The idea is that the deposit address you use buy access to the network forms part of your login credentials. There are no user profiles, no billing accounts, and no commitment; even providing an email address is optional. Each account is designed to be throwaway if you don't want to use it more than once or twice.
